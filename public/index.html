Ap Yantra seva scheme overview



Scheme Name Scheme Name
Launched by Andhra Pradesh Chief Minister, YS Jagan Mohan Reddy
Launched on 26 October 2021
Objective To provide necessary machinery and equipment to the farmers on a rental basis through community hiring centres (CHCs)
Features Tractors, harvesters, sprayers, plows, rotavators, etc. will be provided to the farmers at affordable prices
Eligibility The farmer must be a resident of Andhra Pradesh and a farmer by occupation.
Documents Application form and self-declaration for Aadhaar authentication.
Contact details Toll-free number: 1800-599-1111 (8 am to 8 pm on all working days)




Objective Of AP Yantra Seva Pathakam Scheme

The main objective of the YSR Yantra Seva Pathakam scheme is to provide farm equipment to the farmers on a rental basis. With the implementation of this scheme, farmers will be provided various equipment which will help them in farming activities. This scheme is going to improve the quality of the crops. Other than that farmers will be able to do farming in a larger quantity which will improve the productivity of the farmers. Other than that with the implementation of the Scheme, the income of the farmers will also be increased which will ultimately improve the standard of living for the farmers. This scheme is also going to make farmers self-dependent

Objective Of CHC Tracker

The main objective of CHC Tracker is to track the vehicles going from the Community Hiring Centres (CHC) which are a part of Rythu Bharosa Kendra (RBK). It also facilitate the Streamlining of the Application for requesting for the Supplements from CHC's. This enables the user to apply for the supplements and to enable the best service possible.
